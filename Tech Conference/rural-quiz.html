Rural IT Quiz

The Rural IT Quiz is one of the Flagship Programmes conducted by the Department of IT, BT and S&T, Govt. of Maharastra in partnership with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) since the year 2000, as part of the Event.

For a decade now, the Programme has helped enhance IT awareness amongst Students at the grassroots level, keeping them abreast of the latest developments in the world of Information Technology. The Programme is conducted for Students from Standard 8 - 12. Since 2010, the Programme has moved beyond Maharastra and today has a national footprint to help Students in smaller towns and districts of these States understand technology better.

The Regional Finals of the Programme will be conducted and the winners will now vie for the top honours at the National Finals of the Rural IT Quiz to be conducted on 1st Dec, 2020.
