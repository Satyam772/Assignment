ABOUT MUMBAI TECH CONFERENCE

As the industry’s leading independent event for 20+ years, HR Tech features an unrivaled cutting-edge agenda for HR and IT professionals from businesses of all sizes! With a primary focus on driving HR and IT success through technology, the HR Technology Conference is intended for those looking to continuously optimize usage of current IT systems as well as those looking to buy. It’s where you’ll gain the insight needed to help you make critical HR system decisions that will fuel your business while supporting your organization’s unique needs.

In sessions presented by industry experts and seasoned practitioners, you’ll learn how the right IT technology will help you speed up hiring, attract better candidates, retain top talent, improve performance management and much more. Plus, you’ll gain actionable insight to foster gender diversity to build a stronger workforce and generate better business results in our Women in IT Technology Summit sessions.

What’s more, in the world’s largest Expo of its kind, you’ll be able to see and touch thousands of products from 450+ vendors — ranging from established market leaders to HR startups. There is no better way to see the full capabilities of HR technology as you research and compare products side-by-side, all in one place.

Hailed as “the industry’s annual meeting,” Mumbai Tech Summit 2020 is your once-a-year chance to learn from — and collaborate with — respected thought leaders as well as a community of thousands of your like-minded peers.
